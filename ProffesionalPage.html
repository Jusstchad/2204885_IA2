/career

The career that I would like to have when I leave University is preferably Software Engineer I have conducted research on what are the job requirements for this career which are listed below.

  1. Education
    • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
    • Some positions may require or prefer a Master's degree for advanced roles.
  2. Technical Skills
    • Proficient in programming languages such as Java, Python, C++, or others depending on the job.
    • Experience with software development frameworks and tools.
    • Understanding of data structures and algorithms.
    • Knowledge of web development technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) for front-end roles.
    • Familiarity with databases and SQL for database-related roles.
  3. Experience
    • Hands-on experience with software development projects through internships, personal projects, or contributions to open-source projects.
    • Familiarity with the software development life cycle (SDLC).
  4. Adaptability and Learning
    • Willingness to adapt to new technologies and programming languages.
    • Continuous learning mindset to stay updated with industry trends.
  5. Teamwork
    • Collaboration skills to work efficiently in a team environment.
    • Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git) for collaborative coding.

Qualifications

  1. Certifications
    • Relevant certifications can enhance your profile (e.g., AWS Certified Developer, Microsoft Certified: Azure Developer Associate).
  2. Portfolio
    • Showcase a portfolio of personal or academic projects to demonstrate practical application of skills.
  3. Networking
    • Networking and participating in industry events can help in building connections and staying informed about opportunities.
  4. Soft Skills
    • Time management, organization, and multitasking skills for efficient project execution.
    • Strong work ethic and commitment to deadlines.
