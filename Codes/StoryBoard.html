/theme

The theme of this website is a minimalist kind of computerized theme that is easy to navigate as is show with the backslashes before some headings and the blinking cursor. I chose a dark blue tone with neon green accents to also accentuate the computerized feeling. These colours are also gentle on the eyes which means the website can be viewed for a long period of time.

The website's minimalist computerized theme, featuring a dark blue tone with neon green accents, blinking cursors, and backslashes before headings, is tailored for tech enthusiasts, programmers, and individuals with an interest in futuristic aesthetics. The design, characterized by its simplicity and gentle color scheme, appeals to those who appreciate clean interfaces and can engage with the content for extended periods. Ideal for students, creative professionals, and UX enthusiasts, the site aims to provide a visually stimulating and immersive experience within the realms of technology and design.
